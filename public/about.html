About Game & Grub

Where passion for gaming meets love for great food

Our Story

Game & Grub was born from a simple idea: combining the joy of board gaming with delicious food creates the perfect social experience. Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate gamers and food enthusiasts, we set out to create a space where people could gather, play, and enjoy great meals together.

Our journey began when we realized that traditional restaurants weren't designed for long gaming sessions, and game stores often lacked quality food options. We saw an opportunity to bridge this gap and create something special ‚Äì a place where you could spend hours playing your favorite games without worrying about rushed meals or uncomfortable seating.

Today, Game & Grub is more than just a restaurant with games. We're a community hub where friendships are forged over epic campaigns, families bond over classic board games, and newcomers discover the wonderful world of tabletop gaming. Every dish we serve and every game we stock is chosen with care to enhance your experience.
